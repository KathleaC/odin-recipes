Swamp Potatoes and Sausage

Description

This hearty dish combines tender potatoes with savory sausage, all cooked in a flavorful swamp-inspired sauce. Perfect for a comforting meal any day of the week!

Ingredients

3 Servings

  • 1 1/2 pounds gold potatoes, cut into 1 1/2-inch pieces
  • 1/2 small onion, chopped
  • 3/4 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 3/4 Cajun seasoning
  • 1/2 pound smoked sausage, cut into 1/4-inch slices
  • 1/2 pound fresh green bean, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1/2 packet onion soup mix
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup butter

6 Servings

  • 3 pound gold potatoes, cut into 1 1/2-inch pieces
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 Cajun seasoning
  • 1 pound smoked sausage, cut into 1/4-inch slices
  • 1 pound fresh green bean, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 packet onion soup mix
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 tablespoons butter

9 Servings

  • 4 1/2 pound gold potatoes, cut into 1 1/2-inch pieces
  • 1 1/2 small onion, chopped
  • 2 1/4 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 1/4 Cajun seasoning
  • 1 1/2 pound smoked sausage, cut into 1/4-inch slices
  • 1 1/2 pound fresh green bean, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 1/2 packet onion soup mix
  • 3/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Add potatoes and chopped onion to the bottom of a slow cooker. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and Cajun seasoning. Toss to coat evenly.
  2. Layer sausage slices and green beans on top of the potatoes. Sprinkle with onion soup mix and garlic powder. Dot with butter.
  3. Cover and cook on Low until potatoes are tender and everything is well flavored, 6 to 7 hours, stirring a few times throughout the process and just before serving. Serve warm.
  4. Enjoy!
